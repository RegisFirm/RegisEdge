The Staffing Industry Landscape

The global staffing industry is experiencing robust growth and transformation, driven by specialization, technological advancements, and evolving workforce demands. Regis is well-positioned to capitalize on these trends.

Global Hospitality Staffing Market Growth

The global hospitality staffing market is projected to grow from $971.3 billion in 2022 to $1.88 trillion by 2032, exhibiting a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 6.9%.

Growth in Specialized Staffing Sectors (CAGR)

High-growth areas like Event, Hotel, and Janitorial staffing demonstrate strong demand for specialized talent, trends Regis can strategically leverage.
